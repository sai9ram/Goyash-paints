# GOYASH PAINTS — Website Design System

### Version 1.0

**Design Language:** Modern • Premium • Minimal • Mobile First • Scrollytelling

---

# Design Vision

The website should communicate **Trust, Premium Quality, Innovation, and Reliability**.

Instead of looking like a typical paint company website, the experience should feel similar to premium brands like Asian Paints, Berger, Nerolac, and Dulux while maintaining a unique identity for Goyash Paints.

The design philosophy should emphasize:

* Clean white spaces
* Premium typography
* Smooth animations
* Modern card layouts
* Large product photography
* Scrollytelling experience
* Mobile-first responsive design

# Overall Experience

The website should feel like a premium digital showroom rather than a static product catalogue. Every section should reinforce confidence through clean layouts, generous white space, navy-blue authority, green accents that symbolize quality and sustainability, smooth micro-interactions, and strong calls to action. The user journey should naturally progress from trust and brand story to product discovery, proof of quality, and finally enquiry conversion.
