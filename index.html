Painting Process

Professional Painting Process for a Perfect Finish

A beautiful and durable paint finish begins long before the final coat. Follow Goyash Paints' recommended painting system to ensure maximum adhesion, superior protection, and long-lasting beauty for every wall.

01

Surface Preparation

Begin by cleaning the surface thoroughly to remove dust, dirt, grease, loose particles, and old flaking paint. A clean surface ensures better adhesion and improves the overall durability of the paint system.

02

Crack & Surface Repair

Repair cracks, holes, and uneven areas using suitable fillers. Smooth and level surfaces create the ideal base for an even paint finish and help prevent future surface imperfections.

03

Primer Application

Recommended Product: Goyash Primer

Apply Goyash Primer to seal the surface, improve paint adhesion, and create a strong foundation. Primer enhances coverage while increasing the durability and life of the final paint coat.

04

Putty Application

Apply wall putty to fill minor imperfections and achieve a perfectly smooth surface. Proper putty application improves paint finish, reduces paint consumption, and creates an elegant wall appearance.

05

Paint Application

Recommended Products: Goyash Smart, Veloura, Imperio

Apply two coats of the selected Goyash Paint product to achieve uniform colour, exceptional coverage, rich finish, and long-lasting protection.

06

Final Inspection

After the paint has dried completely, inspect the surface for uniform colour, smooth finish, and complete coverage. The result is a vibrant, durable, and premium-quality finish built to last.
